Due Actions

The Due view collects every non-done action that has a due date set, grouped by urgency so you can see your full commitment picture at a glance.

The four sections

  • Overdue — past the due date; needs attention now
  • Today — due today
  • This week — due within the next 7 days
  • Later — due further out

Within each section actions are sorted by date ascending. Empty sections are hidden — if nothing is overdue, that section simply doesn't appear.

Setting a due date

Open any action (double-click, pencil icon, or Enter key) and fill in the Due: field. Accepted formats:

  • 2026-06-15 — full ISO date
  • 26-6-15 — 2-digit year, single-digit month or day
  • 26-1-2, 26/6/15, 26.6.15 — separators -, / and . all work

Click Clear to remove the due date. The action disappears from Due view and the Due column goes blank in other panels.

Due pressure in other panels

A Due column also appears in Next Actions, Backlog, Tag filter, and Saved Views — so you see due pressure in context without switching views.

What Due is not

Due is date pressure, not scheduling. NAM does not set reminders, create calendar events, or reschedule recurring work. Calendar integration remains an external concern.
