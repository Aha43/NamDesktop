Getting Things Done (GTD)

GTD is a productivity method by David Allen. The central insight: your mind is for having ideas, not holding them. Capture everything that has your attention into a trusted system, so you can focus fully on what you're doing right now.

The five steps:

  1. Capture — collect everything into an Inbox
  2. Clarify — decide what each item is and what the next action is
  3. Organise — put it in the right place (Next Actions, Projects, Someday…)
  4. Reflect — review your lists regularly
  5. Engage — do the work

NamDesktop implements these steps directly: Inbox → clarify (Process…) → Next Actions or Projects → review → do.
