Inbox

The Inbox is your capture bucket — a place to dump anything that has your attention before you've decided what it is or what to do about it.

The rule: capture first, decide later. When something lands on your radar, put it in the inbox and move on. Deciding what to do with it (processing) is a separate step.

Processing an inbox item means asking: What is this, and what's the next action?

  • If it's a single action → convert to a Next Action
  • If it needs multiple steps → convert to a Project
  • If it's reference material → delete it or file it elsewhere
  • If it's not actionable → delete it

Aim to process your inbox to zero at least once a week. A full inbox is a sign that decisions are being deferred — it creates mental load.

Age column

Each inbox row shows an Age value — how long since the item was last touched (3d, 2w, 4m…). Items older than 7 days turn amber: a signal that processing has been skipped too long. Blank means the item has no timestamp yet.

Clearing several at once

Select multiple rows — click then Shift+click for a range, or Cmd/Ctrl+click to pick individual items — then right-click and choose Delete selected (N). You'll be asked to confirm before they're all removed in one step. Right-clicking a single row still gives the full menu (Process, Rename, Mark done, Delete).

Sort order

The Sort button in the toolbar cycles through three states:

  • Sort (inactive) — items appear in capture order
  • Oldest — oldest items first; good for clearing what has waited longest
  • Newest — most recent first; useful when triaging a burst of captures

The sort preference is saved and restored when you reopen the app.
