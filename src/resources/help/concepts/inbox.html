Inbox

The Inbox is your capture bucket — a place to dump anything that has your attention before you've decided what it is or what to do about it.

The rule: capture first, decide later. When something lands on your radar, put it in the inbox and move on. Deciding what to do with it (processing) is a separate step.

Processing an inbox item means asking: What is this, and what's the next action?

  • If it's a single action → convert to a Next Action
  • If it needs multiple steps → convert to a Project
  • If it's reference material → delete it or file it elsewhere
  • If it's not actionable → delete it

Aim to process your inbox to zero at least once a week. A full inbox is a sign that decisions are being deferred — it creates mental load.
