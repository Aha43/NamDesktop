Moon Cards

Moon Cards is a distraction-free execution mode that presents one action at a time from any lens view. The planning is already done — the lens is the plan. Moon Cards is just a different way to consume it.

Click the deck icon on the Next Actions, Backlog, or any Saved View panel to enter deck mode. You see one card at a time: the action title, its description, and the project path. Press Space or click Done to mark it complete and advance to the next. Use ← → to browse without completing. Press Esc to exit back to the table.

The deck answers one question: what now?

The name

Inspired by the Apollo mission cuff checklists — the small spiral-bound cards astronauts wore on their wrists during moonwalks. Each card held exactly one procedure: no more, no less. When you are executing, you should not be navigating a bloated app. You should be getting things done.
