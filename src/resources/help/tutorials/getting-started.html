Getting Started with NamDesktop

NamDesktop is a personal productivity app built around Getting Things Done (GTD). The core idea is simple: keep your mind clear by capturing everything, then deciding what to do with it later.

Step 1 — Capture to your Inbox

The Inbox is your capture bucket. When something lands on your radar — a task, an idea, a nagging thing — put it in the inbox. Don't think about it yet, just capture it.

Click Inbox in the sidebar, then Add item.

Step 2 — Process your Inbox

Processing means deciding what each inbox item actually is. Right-click any inbox item and choose Process…:

  • Next action — it's a single concrete step you can take right now. It moves to Next Actions.
  • Project — it needs more than one action to complete. It moves to Projects.

Aim to process your inbox to zero regularly.

Step 3 — Work your Next Actions list

The Next Actions view shows everything you've committed to doing. Pick one and do it. When it's done, right-click → Mark done.

Step 4 — Use Backlog for deferred work

Not everything needs to happen today. The Backlog holds actions that are real but not yet the right time. Move things there deliberately — it keeps Next Actions uncluttered and trustworthy.

Step 5 — Review weekly

Once a week: empty your inbox, scan your projects, move stale next actions to backlog, and promote backlog items that are now relevant.

What's next?

Once you're comfortable with the basics, explore Tag filter for filtering by tag, Goal Board for high-level goal tracking, Focus mode for working through a list one action at a time, and Git sync for backing up your workspace to GitHub, and AI assistant for managing your workspace through conversation.
